# 💸 BrokeBuddy

**Your AI-powered financial survival guide for students**

BrokeBuddy is not just an expense tracker — it’s a **gamified financial companion** that transforms money into real-life decisions, habits, and future outcomes.

---

## 🚀 Problem

Students struggle with:
- Running out of money before month-end
- Impulsive spending habits
- Lack of financial awareness
- No personalized financial guidance

Traditional apps only track expenses — they don’t help users **understand or improve behavior**.

---

## 💡 Solution

BrokeBuddy introduces a **living financial system**:

- Converts money into relatable terms  
  👉 “You have 5 chai ☕ and 2 dosa 🥞 left this month”

- Predicts financial future  
  👉 “You’ll run out in 6 days”

- Provides AI-powered guidance  
  👉 Smarter spending suggestions, subscription alerts

- Makes finance interactive and emotional through gamification

---

## 🎮 Key Features

### 🐱 Pixel Companion
- Customizable pixel cat
- Reacts to spending behavior
- Click interaction (meow, jump)

---

### ⚡ Financial Health System
Game-like stats:
- Balance (💰)
- Spending Habit
- Risk Level
- Financial Discipline
- Savings Progress

---

### 📊 Smart Dashboard
- Real-time balance insights
- Spending breakdown
- Predictive alerts

---

### 🤖 AI Assistant
- “Can I afford this?”
- Personalized financial advice
- Behavior-based recommendations

---

### 🎧 Monthly Wrapped
- Spotify-style financial summary
- Spending patterns
- Biggest expenses
- Habit insights

---

### 💀 Critical Event System
- When balance hits zero:
  👉 Pixel duck triggers “Uh-oh! Balance Over 💀”

---

### 📈 Growth & Investment Nudges
- Suggests SIPs, savings habits
- Visualizes long-term impact

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** Lovable / Stitch (Pixel UI-based mobile interface)
- **Backend:** Supabase (Database + Authentication)
- **AI Layer:** Vercel (Serverless functions + AI logic)
